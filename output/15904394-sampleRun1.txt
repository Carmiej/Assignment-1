Welcome to my game Monsters And Dungeons?
Your objective is to move throgh the maze and find the exit in one if the rooms and have atleast $500 gold.
Each room will have a random amount of gold from 1-500 and each monster will have 250.
There is also a dagger in one of the rooms to help you in your endevours.
Caution - every command you type will have a 4 in 20 chance to spawn a monster.
So be strategic and plan your moves.
Goodluck and may the odds be ever in your favour
-----------------------------------------------------------------
To play the game use the commands:
open door n - (to open the door marked n)
pickup n - (to pickup items n)
help - to see avaliable commands
When in battlemode use the commands:
attack - to attack the monster.
This game is hardcore there is no running from the monsters.
